Portfolio 5

Goal

The goal of this portfolio piece was to create a figure for my thesis that displayed the effect on my intervention on how much parents prioritized a diverse school.

Parents who were presented with a values-alignment intervention focusing on positioning diversity as a means to fufill academic achievement chose a diverse school more often than parents in the control condition. Initally, parents in both condition claimed to value diversity in their children’s education.
